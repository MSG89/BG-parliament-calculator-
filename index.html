    Welcome to the Bulgarian parliament elections calculator website !

    You can use one of our calculators to dry run elections for the Bulgarian national assembly with different inputs.
    Please hover your cursor over Tools button in the navbar and select one of the calculators.

    Bulgarian parliament calculator based on percentage of votes won per party

    In the following field you can input the parties you want to dry run an election.
    You can either use the default ones or change them to your preference.
    Total percentage has to be less than 100 and at least one party has to have more than 4 percent.
    Once ready, click calculate button to get the results for which party gets in the parliament.
    Use the additional buttons to either check for possible coalitions or reset view back to the initial screen.
    You can click on Home in the navbar to go back to the Homepage.
